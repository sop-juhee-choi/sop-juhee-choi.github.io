A dramatic coloratura soprano with a brilliant timbre and a wide vocal range, Juhee Choi graduated from the Department of Vocal Music, College of Music, Seoul National University, and received her M.A. degree from the Graduate School. She later completed the COP (Certificate of Performance) program in Vocal Performance at the Vienna Music and Arts Private University of the City of Vienna (MUK), Austria.

While studying in the graduate program at Seoul National University, she gained recognition by winning First Prize at the 8th French Art Song Competition hosted by the French Art Song Society. Since then, she has appeared dozens of times in concerts presented by the French Art Song Society and Paris Ensemble, continuing to perform French mélodie.

Studying under Prof. Se-Won Park, she developed a strong interest in opera, beginning with her performance as the lead role ‘Gilda’ in the Seoul City Opera’s outreach production of Rigoletto.

She has since maintained an active performance career, including appearances as ‘Annina’ in the Seoul Philharmonic Orchestra Masterpiece Series (conducted by Myung-Whun Chung) concert opera production of La Traviata; as a soprano soloist in an invited concert with the Gangnam Symphony Orchestra (conducted by Hyun-Seok Seo); as the lead role ‘Violetta’ in the Daehangno Opera Festival production of La Traviata; and as ‘Musetta’ in an opera gala concert of La Bohème. She also presented the 28th Kumho Young Artist Solo Recital hosted by the Kumho Asiana Cultural Foundation.

As a member of Gil Opera, she has performed the lead role ‘Violetta’ in Father Germont on multiple occasions.

Alongside these activities, she has continued to pursue artistic growth and sought admission to MUK in Vienna. In an audition before the world-renowned soprano Prof. Melba Ramos, she received high praise for her performance of the Queen of the Night aria from Mozart’s opera Die Zauberflöte, and was subsequently admitted to the university.

After returning to Korea, she successfully performed the lead role ‘Gilda’ in the concert opera Rigoletto presented by Scala Opera.

In Korea, she studied with Se-Won Park, Jung-A Kim, Young-Hwa Lee, Seung-Hyun Lee, and Min-Jung Kim, and in Europe she studied with Melba Ramos. Juhee Choi is currently an active performer as a member of the Seoul Art Song Research Society and Real Stimme (German Art Song Society). She also teaches in the Vocal Expert Program at the Lifelong Education Center of Korea Baptist Theological University and serves as an instructor at Kaywon Arts High School.
